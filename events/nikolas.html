Event Significance

This event contributed to my learning by connecting electrical engineering concepts with real-world applications. It showed how technical knowledge can be used to improve systems, communities, infrastructure, sustainability, and professional practice.

Knowledge Gained

Key Takeaways

1Solar-powered automation can solve household and community problems while reducing energy consumption.
2Disaster resilience requires integrated systems such as solar PV, battery storage, and water purification.
3Energy management in schools can improve safety, reduce operating costs, and support long-term sustainability.
